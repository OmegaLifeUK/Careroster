What Makes Us Different

AI that actually
understands care

Legacy care software digitises paper. Care One OS thinks alongside your team — generating care plans, predicting incidents, optimising schedules, and answering questions in real time.

Care Copilot

An AI assistant embedded in the sidebar. Ask it anything — "What's Mrs Johnson's medication history?" — and get instant, context-aware answers from your own data.

AI Care Plan Generator

Feed it an assessment and it drafts a full care plan — person-centred, CQC-compliant, and ready for review. Hours of work reduced to minutes.

Predictive Incident Alerts

Analyses daily logs, mood patterns, and behavioural trends to flag potential incidents before they happen. Proactive care, not reactive paperwork.

AI Document Importer

Upload a PDF referral, assessment, or discharge letter. AI extracts the data and populates client records automatically — no manual re-keying.

AI Schedule Optimiser

Matches staff to shifts based on skills, availability, continuity of care, and travel distance. Fills gaps intelligently instead of guesswork.

Intelligent Reporting

Ask for a report in plain English — "Show me all incidents this quarter by category" — and get it instantly. No report builder needed.
