Real-Time Dashboard

The Care One OS dashboard aggregates data across all modules in real time. Managers see active clients, staff attendance, compliance scores, overdue tasks, and alerts — all in one view. No more logging into 5 different systems to understand what's happening in your service.

AI Care Plan Generator

Upload a referral PDF, assessment, or discharge letter. The AI Document Importer extracts all relevant data — demographics, medical history, current medications, risk factors — and populates client records automatically.

The AI Care Plan Generator then drafts a full person-centred care plan that's CQC-compliant and ready for clinical review. Staff review and approve — the AI does the heavy lifting, humans make the decisions.

Medication Management

Digital MAR sheets replace paper records. Every medication round is tracked in real time — dose administered, time, staff member, and witness for controlled drugs. PRN medications require a documented reason. Error reporting is built in. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Predictive Incident Alerts

The AI analyses daily logs, mood patterns, food intake, mobility data, and behavioural trends to identify residents at elevated risk. It flags potential incidents before they happen — giving staff time to intervene, consult with GPs, and adjust care plans proactively rather than reactively.

Care Copilot

An AI assistant embedded in the sidebar. Ask it anything in plain English — "What's Mrs Johnson's medication history?", "Show me overdue DBS checks", "Which staff are trained in PEG feeding?" — and get instant, context-aware answers from your own data. Every AI feature is built on your own data. Nothing leaves your organisation. Fully GDPR compliant.
